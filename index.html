About HHWSF

Healing Hearts Widows Support Foundation (HHWSF) is a non-profit, non-governmental international charity working to promote and protect the rights and welfare of widows. Founded in 2008 and registered in Nigeria under Part C of the Corporate Affairs Commission with its head office in Enugu, Nigeria. The Foundation is a broad-based initiative that assists widows and their children to achieve economic self-sufficiency through empowerment skills, access to decent and affordable housing, free health care services, support services, pro bono legal services, training, and interest-free loans.

Mission

We empower widows and advocate for their rights by providing free medical aid, legal assistance, financial support, and educational opportunities. Through sensitization, scholarships, and community outreach, we promote dignity, equity, and sustainable opportunities for widows in Nigeria.

Vision

A society with zero tolerance for abuse and violations of the rights of widows and their children‚Äîwhere they are protected and adequately provided for.
