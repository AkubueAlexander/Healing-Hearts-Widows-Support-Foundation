About Us

Founded in 2008, HHWSF is a registered non-profit organization in Nigeria dedicated to protecting the rights and welfare of widows and their children. Our work includes empowerment programs, medical aid, education, housing support, and advocacy.

Mission

Empower widows through healthcare, legal aid, education, and sustainable support systems.

Vision

A society with zero tolerance for widow abuse and violations—where dignity and opportunity thrive.

Our Programs

Immediate relief and long-term empowerment initiatives for widows and their families.

Medical Aid

Free health clinics, maternal care, and referrals.

Legal Support

Pro bono legal aid for inheritance rights and abuse cases.

Empowerment

Skills training, microloans, and small business support.

Education

Scholarships and school support for children.

Housing

Access to decent and affordable housing support.

Advocacy

Community outreach and sensitization campaigns.
